Meet the owner

Drew Riley

Owner & technician, Climate Crafter

Climate Crafter was created to fill a gap. My father was an honest, hard-working man who operated on a simple principle — if you work hard and the work is top quality, everything else will fall into place. Anything worth doing is worth doing right. That's what we're built on.

"No gimmicks, no shifty promotions. We want to give you the knowledge and service you expect from a professional."

Drew Riley, owner

I worked in construction into my 20s before heading to college, where I earned a degree in Electrical Engineering. That background gave me a deep understanding of how systems work and, just as importantly, how to tell when something is being misdiagnosed or unnecessarily replaced.

Eventually I realized that if you're not part of the solution, you're part of the problem. Climate Crafter exists to give you straight information and help you make the best decision for your home and your budget. No upsells, no unnecessary repairs — just good work done right.
